Boats Fun Facts



1 - Large boats are generally called ships.

2 - Boats and ships serve a variety of purposes including transport, recreation, fishing, sporting competition and military operations.

3 - Sailboats are propelled by wind and sails.

4 - Cargo ships carry goods between ports around the world.

5 - Some boats are human powered, including kayaks, canoes and gondolas.

Submarine Fun Facts



1 - Submarines are a special type of watercraft that can operate underwater.

2 - Submarines are used by the military as part of navy forces, protecting aircraft carriers, performing reconnaissance and carrying out other tasks.

3 - Some submarines can remain submerged for months at a time.

4 - Submarines use ballast tanks to hold water, allowing them to submerge when necessary.

5 - Submarines typically feature a raised tower which holds radar equipment, the periscope and other systems.
