Buses Fun Facts



1 - Before the 1920's buses used cloth curtains instead of glass windows!

2 - School-bus-yellow has been compared to the color of mangoes.

3 - In the 1930's truck designs were being modified to make school buses.

4 - The largest bus in the world holds 300 passengers! It is in China and is 24 meters long (82 feet); normal buses are around 19 meters in length (64 feet).

5 - School buses are now all equipped with stop signs that pop open when they stop. This warns drivers to stay put while children are boarding of off-loading.

Cars Fun Facts



1 - The first Japanese car in the US was the Honda Accord, made in 1982.

2 - The first cars used a lever instead of a steering wheel.

3 - Airbags only take 40 milliseconds to inflate during an accident.

4 - The very first car was made that was powered by fuel, was made in 1807.

5 - Cars are the most recycled product in the world. Approximately 95% of retired cars are recycled. About 27 million cars are recycled each year.
